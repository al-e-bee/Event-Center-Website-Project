How to Book Your Event

  1. Inquiry: Fill out our contact form with your event details and preferred dates.
  2. Consultation: Meet with our Event Director, Isabelle, to tour the venue and discuss your vision.
  3. Proposal: Receive a customized quote and floor plan tailored to your needs.
  4. Reservation: Sign the agreement and submit your deposit to lock in your date!
